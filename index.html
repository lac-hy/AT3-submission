MELBOURNE AS A SOUNDSCAPE©

EXPLORING MELBOURNE’S
RHYTHM THROUGH SOUND COMM2753

The project transforms Melbourne's Landmarks into an immersive experience.

I wanted to explore how different parts of a city carry their own distinct sonic identities, how the sounds of movement, architecture, and atmosphere shape our perception of place. Through an experimental approach, I aimed to translate these urban sound textures into an interactive experience, allowing users to listen to the city as a living composition. The project became a way to express the relationship between environment and emotion through sound, design, and digital interaction.
